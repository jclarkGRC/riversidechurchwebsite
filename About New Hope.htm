A little bit about New Hope

New Hope Free Will Baptist Church is affiliated with the  National Association of Free Will Baptists which has a fellowship of over 2,400 churches in 42 states and 14 nations around the world. But more importantly the members of our local congregation meet to worship God and fellowship with each other every Sunday morning. We would like to invite you to join us!
