Introduction:

The following is a consideration of and an attempt to answer some questions about the place Christians usually refer to as "heaven". The format will be different from our usual studies. We will use a design which will consist mainly of a series of questions. There will be comments to provoke thought and discussion, and, of course, many Bible references as we will be using God's Word as the primary source of knowledge for our investigation.

I also want to acknowledge Randy Alcorn's book entitled Heaven published in 2004 by Tyndale House Publishers. When originally considering these questions as part of the series Tough Questions for Christians in 2009, I relied heavily on his work and highly recommend it for deeper study on this subject.

So ... with this brief introduction and understanding ... let's begin!
