Conclusion:

I suppose it is unnecessary but we should probably emphasize that the idea that Scientology and Christianity are compatible is nonsense. We saw many reasons throughout the course of the study. Here are just a few in summary:

  1. Scientology is a meshing of several philosophies, including black magic occultism, Eastern mysticism, the concept of reincarnation along with a healthy dose of crazy science-fiction thrown in for good measure. Obviously, these things do not mix well with Christianity.
  2. Hubbard is on record saying man is basically good and can save himself while Christians believe the Bible when it says we are all fallen sinners who need a Savior.
  3. Hubbard taught that man (the thetan) willed himself and MEST into being while Christians believe the Bible when it says God created everything that exists, including mankind.

We should also mention that Scientology has its tentacles out trying to grab money through more than just the Church of Scientology label. A partial listing of other front organizations (some of which we have already seen) includes:

  • Sylvan Learning Centers;
  • Sterling Management Systems;
  • Way to Happiness Foundation;
  • Citizens Commission on Human Rights;
  • Concerned Businessman’s Association of America;
  • HealthMed;
  • Criminon;
  • Narcanon.
There may well be others. It is just a good and prudent practice to do a thorough check of any organization you are considering joining or supporting to make sure you are not unknowingly helping a cause you do not condone.

Beyond that, I am almost at a loss at this point about how to conclude. A friend, Richard Berghammer, commented to me that Scientology was one of the more bizarre philosophies he had ever encountered. I did not pay much heed to that until I started investigating it for myself. It baffles me as to how anyone can fall for such an obviously flawed and fraudulent system ... except that it shows just how desperate some people are for that which will make their lives seem better, or purposeful, or worthwhile. We as Christians have the answer for that, namely, a real relationship with an almighty, holy Creator God through the atonement and mediation of His only Son, Jesus Christ. Let’s make sure we are as dedicated to sharing the Lord with others with a motive of love as Scientologists are about recruiting others for Dianetic auditing with a longing for money.
