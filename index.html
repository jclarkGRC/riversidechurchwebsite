Welcome to New Hope Free Will Baptist Church in Sumner, Washington – a warm fellowship of people gathering together to worship God, love each other, and make disciples for the Lord.

We believe the Bible to be God's inerrant, infallible word to mankind. God gave it to us so that we might come to know and be reconciled with Him and His Son Jesus. We teach and preach the Bible so people may experience salvation for the first time and also so those who know the Lord can discover more about Him and His will for their lives.

So whether you have questions about God and are seeking answers or you have been a believer for a while, you are most welcome to come join us for services. We look forward to meeting you!

Come be a part of the family!
